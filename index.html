About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed online survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and see how long you can survive in this challenging adventure.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Attacks are automatic - just position yourself strategically to defeat the monsters. Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new upgrades to enhance your abilities.

Multiple Characters

Choose from various unique characters, each with their own special abilities and play styles.

Upgrade System

Level up and select powerful upgrades to create your own unique build and combat strategy.

Endless Challenge

Test your skills against increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival mode.

Game Tips

Focus on movement speed early to avoid being surrounded. Don't neglect area damage upgrades as the monster waves get denser. Pick up all experience orbs to level up faster and unlock more powerful abilities.
